Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use Spendly, we collect the expense data you enter — including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions. We do not collect payment information or any financial account details.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Spendly service — displaying your expenses, generating summaries, and enabling filtering. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app for you.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We apply industry-standard practices to protect your information from unauthorised access. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not sell or share your personal data with third parties. We may use trusted infrastructure providers (such as hosting services) to operate the app, but these providers are contractually prohibited from using your data for any other purpose.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us. We are committed to addressing your concerns promptly and transparently.
